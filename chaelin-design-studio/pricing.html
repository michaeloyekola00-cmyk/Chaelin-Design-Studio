Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, standard projects are split into a 50% deposit and 50% upon completion. For larger packages, we can discuss multi-month payment structures.

How long does a project take?

Brand identities typically take 3-4 weeks. Websites take 4-6 weeks. Timelines depend largely on prompt feedback and content readiness.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just an understanding of your business goals! We’ll provide a discovery questionnaire to guide the rest of the information gathering.

Can I start with one service and add more later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Brand Identity and upgrade to add a website or a graphic design retainer down the line.

What if I need something not listed here?

Get in touch. We can scope custom projects tailored exactly to your unique requirements.
