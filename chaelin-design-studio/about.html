Our Philosophy

We believe every business
deserves a brand that works.

Chaelin Design Studio is a London-based creative studio built for founders and businesses that are done apologising for how they look. We build brand identities, design systems, and websites that give businesses the visual confidence to charge what they are worth, attract who they want, and show up like the real thing — because they are.

How It Started

Built from experience,
not theory.

Chaelin Design Studio grew out of Chaelin Studios — a book marketing agency that has spent years helping authors build audiences and communicate their work to the world. We understand what it means for a brand to carry a message.

We know how much is lost when the visual identity does not match the quality of what is actually being offered. This studio exists to close that gap — for businesses at every stage.
